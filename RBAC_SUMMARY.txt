AUTHORIZATION RULE (IMPLEMENTED)
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
User can perform action X at location L if:

  EXISTS assignment WHERE:
    (scope_type = 'BUSINESS' AND role has permission_key)
    OR
    (scope_type = 'LOCATION' AND location_id = L AND role has permission_key)
